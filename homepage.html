About Us

Our story began with a simple dream: to create a space where exceptional coffee meets authentic Balinese hospitality. From bean to cup, we're passionate about sourcing and brewing the finest Indonesian coffee.

Our Coffee Story

🌱 Single Origin Sourcing

We partner directly with local farmers across Indonesia's coffee regions, ensuring fair trade and exceptional quality from farm to cup.

☕ Expert Roasting

Our master roasters carefully develop each bean's unique flavor profile, highlighting the distinct characteristics of Indonesian terroir.

🎯 Precision Brewing

Using traditional and modern brewing methods, we extract the perfect balance of flavors to create your ideal cup.
